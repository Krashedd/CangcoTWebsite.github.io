About Us

Welcome to Yellow Hill Leisure Resort, an exclusive retreat perched atop the mountains where nature meets luxury. Since our grand opening on August 28, 2022, we have been dedicated to offering a joyful vacation experience in the serene hillside. Our resort features a stunning pool and two beautifully designed A Houses, perfect for relaxation and rejuvenation. Here, the early morning breeze and breathtaking views create the ideal setting for genuine smiles and unforgettable moments. Whether you're lounging by the pool or exploring the surrounding landscapes, Yellow Hill promises a unique blend of comfort and natural beauty. Our commitment to excellence ensures that every stay is memorable and refreshing. At Yellow Hill, we cherish the tranquility of mountain life and invite you to escape the ordinary. Enjoy a stay where every detail is crafted to enhance your connection with nature. Capture the essence of peace and joy in every photo, as you embrace the serene charm of our hillside paradise. Come experience the magic of Yellow Hill Leisure Resort and make memories that last a lifetime.!
