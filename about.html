About Us

Our Story

Yellow Hill Leisure Resort began with a dream to create a mountain escape where serenity and luxury meet. Since opening on August 28, 2022, we’ve been dedicated to providing a unique retreat with breathtaking views and a peaceful atmosphere. Our resort features a stunning pool and two charming A Houses, designed for relaxation and enjoyment. Inspired by the invigorating mountain breeze and the beauty of our surroundings, Yellow Hill is a place where genuine smiles and unforgettable moments are created. Our team is passionate about offering exceptional service and a memorable stay, ensuring every visit is a cherished experience.

Our Philosophy

At Yellow Hill Leisure Resort, our philosophy is to blend luxury with nature, offering a peaceful retreat that rejuvenates and inspires. We believe in creating more than just a stay; we aim to provide an experience where elegance meets simplicity. Embracing our hillside location, we focus on genuine hospitality and memorable moments, ensuring that each guest enjoys both comfort and tranquility. Our commitment is to craft an environment where nature and relaxation come together seamlessly.

What We Offer

At Yellow Hill Leisure Resort, we provide a serene mountain retreat with a stunning pool and two elegant A Houses. Our dedicated team ensures genuine hospitality and memorable experiences in a tranquil hillside setting. Enjoy the perfect blend of luxury and nature, with peaceful mornings and enchanting evenings designed for ultimate relaxation.

Our Promise

At Yellow Hill Leisure Resort, we promise exceptional comfort and genuine hospitality in a stunning natural setting. We are dedicated to creating unforgettable experiences, ensuring each stay is relaxing and rejuvenating.
